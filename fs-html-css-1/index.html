Po the Dragon Warrior

The dragon warrior Po with his friends and their master fight to stop the villain.

About

Po might just be the laziest, clumsiest panda in the Valley of Peace,buthe secretly dreams of becoming a kung fu legend. When the villainous snow leopard Tai Lung threatens Po's homeland, the hapless panda is chosen to fulfil an ancient prophecy and defend the Valley from attack. Training under Master Shifu, Po embarks on an epic high-kicking adventure as he sets out to thwart Tai Lung's evil plans. It is a DreamWorks animation.
